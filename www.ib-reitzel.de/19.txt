An overview of the company's history: The Georgi Reitzel engineering office was founded on April 1, 1962 by Georgi Reitzel in Gundernhausen. Georgi Reitzel had previously worked as a planner for the American armed forces since 1949. Until the early 1990s, planning contracts for military buildings and civil engineering accounted for around half of the business volume - for the US armed forces as well as for NATO and many state building authorities in the Federal Republic of Germany. In 1973 the company moved to Groß -zimmer, today's company headquarters. 1985 Michael Reitzel joins the office after completing his studies. On April 1, 1995, the engineering firm Georgi Reitzel was transferred to the engineering firm Reitzel GbR mbH through a partnership agreement. The owners are Georgi and Michael Reitzel. As of January 1, 2004, the legal form is changed to a GmbH and Co KG Georgi Reitzel died on June 23, 2008 at the age of 78. Michael Reitzel has been the sole managing director since then.
