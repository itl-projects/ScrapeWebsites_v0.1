KYB is Japan's largest hydraulic equipment industry. The KYB group, with 11,370 employees worldwide, invoices over $ 2.6 billion, of which 60% comes from the sale of shock absorbers for automotive use. In its 15 factories located in Asia , Europe and the United States, KYB has an annual production capacity of over 75 million shock absorbers. KYB has the largest shock absorber factory in the world (in Gifu, Japan), with a production capacity of over 50 million pieces. This plant is highly automated and can easily switch from production of one type to another in 15 seconds.KYB Corporation is listed on the Tokyo stock exchange and exports its products to more than 100 countries around the world.KYB Europe is based near Dusseldorf in Germany, has 7 branches, and sells and provides assistance throughout Europe and Africa.
