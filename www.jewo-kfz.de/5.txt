The inspection and oil change are usually carried out at specific intervals specified by the manufacturer or the mileage. The scope of services of the small and large inspection, which are important for your vehicle, can usually be found in the service booklet of your vehicle manufacturer. Flexible intervals - vehicle specification of inspection with oil change In newer vehicles, the intervals between inspections are flexible. Based on calculations that are specified by the manufacturer, the load or wear limit is recognized and the driver is asked to visit his workshop for the next inspection with an oil change. For this purpose, measured values ​​that are directly related to the driving style are used, such as speed and operating temperature.
