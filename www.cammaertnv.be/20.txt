Cammaert Trucks has specialized in the sale, maintenance and repairs of IVECO Trucks and FIAT Professional for more than 50 years. Thanks to our excellent service, we have become a household name in the transport world. The central location of our four locations in Gooik, Temse, Antwerp & Sint-Lievens-Houtem around the three major traffic axes between Antwerp, Brussels and Ghent ensures that we can serve our customers quickly. Extensive range of commercial vehicles & wide variety of customers At Cammaert Trucks you can find a very extensive range of IVECO and Fiat Professional commercial vehicles: from the smallest van to the heavy Stralis Hi-Way. Our customer portfolio includes both small self-employed persons, SMEs and large transport companies from various sectors. “Service is our strategy, customization is our passion” As a family business, we attach great importance to the personal relationships with our customers. Providing service and customization is one of our top priorities to meet your needs. With our four locations and an efficient sub-dealer network, our company is easily accessible for service and maintenance. Flexibility is one of our greatest assets in combination with the professional knowledge of our employees. However, we do not limit ourselves to the rolling stock, but we are also happy to look for suitable trailers, cranes, loading bridges and the like for you. In this way we offer you an individualized total solution. More than 50 years of know-how in the service and sales of commercial vehicles are our guarantee for the best price, quality and service. Service is our strategy, customization is our passion. Cammaert Trucks stands for individualized total solutions for every customer.
