Ride farther and longer with TOTAL Diesel Excellium Home Fuels Professionals Ride farther and longer with TOTAL Diesel Excellium In transport and logistics, or for a fleet manager, the performance of an engine and its preservation in time is a factor. To support these businesses, TOTAL has developed a premium quality fuel, efficient and engine-friendly. TOTAL Diesel Excellium supports your activity, allowing you to go further and consume better. In order to support transport professionals in the development of their activities as well as fleet managers in the management of their fleet, TOTAL has developed a generation of premium fuels. TOTAL Diesel Excellium is a diesel fuel that contains a series of additives that guarantee superior performance and permanently preserve the engine of your vehicles. For more performance, a fuel incorporating the best additives Among the additives contained in the TOTAL Excellium product range, we find: friction modifiers; agents for better combustion; anti-foam; anticorrosion; detergents; agents improving cold resistance. Take advantage of the many advantages offered by TOTAL Diesel Excellium The additives present in the TOTAL Excellium product range help to considerably improve the fuel properties. For professionals, in transport for example, or for vehicle fleet managers, the advantages are numerous. This new generation of fuels cleans your engine and protects it durably to make it more efficient, more economical and less polluting. TOTAL Diesel Excellium also guarantees easy start-up and start-up of vehicles. Save on fuel costs TOTAL Diesel Excellium guarantees better combustion. This allows savings on the fuel item of up to 4.4% compared to a fuel that is not specifically additive. The integrated additives keep the engines clean and reduce the energy losses linked to friction in the engine. Reduce the risk of breakdowns, preserve the value of your vehicles These are the general costs of managing and operating your fleet which can also be reduced by using TOTAL Diesel Excellium. The new generation of fuel protects engines from wear and tear and storage facilities. Optimized combustion reduces the level of fouling of the engine and surrounding elements. You reduce the risk of breakdowns, such as those related to clogging of filters at low temperatures, as well as engine maintenance costs. Decrease the impact of your activity on the environment Thanks to its composition, TOTAL Diesel Excellium contributes to the reduction of polluting and CO2 emissions. The other advantages of TOTAL Diesel Excellium TOTAL Diesel Excellium is an all-in-one product. In other words, there is no need to add other additives, even in special situations. The fuel contains an anti-foaming agent, protects the components of the injection system against the risk of rust, or against accidental pollution of water in the fuel. For best performance, choose TOTAL Diesel Excellium at our service stations.
