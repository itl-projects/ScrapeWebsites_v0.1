Our advice What is the Proxifuel subsidiary? The ProxiFuel subsidiary is a 100% Total subsidiary. It is made up of 10 regional centers located in the major cities of Belgium. Thanks to this subsidiary, we remain close to our customers and it is for this purpose that we opted for local proximity centers rather than a single call center in Belgium. Do all Proxifuel regional centers and resellers offer the same service? No, our resellers and our regional centers may not offer the same services, we invite you to contact your usual fuel oil distributor to find out what services they offer. In my old boiler, can I put Total Ultra fuel oil? TOTAL Mazout Ultra is the high quality fuel oil developed by Total, i, offers you better performance and better protection for your boiler. It is compatible with all equipment on the market, new or old. No particular adjustment is necessary to benefit from the advantages of TOTAL Fuel Oil Ultra. To fully benefit from the advantages of TOTAL Mazout Ultra from the first delivery, we recommend that you: Ensure that your storage tank has been cleaned for less than 10 years. Turn off your boiler and turn it back on approximately 2 hours after delivery. Keep your storage as full as possible. Purge as often as necessary to remove the condensation water from the air that enters the tank when it empties. I would like to order my fuel oil online, how can I do this? To order your fuel oil online, we invite you to visit the website https://www.mazout-on-line.be. You will be able to compare the price offers of Total fuel oil distributors (with our Proxifuel subsidiary included) in your city, place an order and even pay online directly if you wish.
