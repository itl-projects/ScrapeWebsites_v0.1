Save money on heating with our multi-energy offer Home My house Save energy Save money on heating with our multi-energy offer Consuming efficiently, at low cost, while respecting the environment today involves better combine the available energies. An optimal energy mix will combine renewable sources, such as solar panels, 100% green electricity or pellet, and traditional energies, such as natural gas and heating oil. The diversity of TOTAL's activities in the energy sector positions us as the ideal partner to meet your heating needs. One of the challenges for consumers who want to save energy while preserving the environment is a better combination of the energy sources available to them depending on their situation. Take advantage of the best energy mix with TOTAL A good energy mix will notably rely on a share of renewable energy and a share of domestic production, with the installation of solar panels for example. It also protects you from large variations in market energy prices. With its multi-energy offer, TOTAL is your benchmark supplier for composing your energy mix: Pellet, energy that respects the environment Total Pellets Premium wood pellets come from the forests of Ardenne and the surrounding area, all managed by in a sustainable way. TOTAL pellet is made from softwood sawdust, free from any chemical treatment. This makes it 100% natural energy and particularly respectful of our environment. In your home, a pellet stove or boiler can very well meet your heating and hot water needs. Combine traditional heating and solar panels Today, everyone can become an energy producer, by installing solar, thermal and / or photovoltaic panels in their homes, or by using a heat pump. Depending on the situation of each, it is interesting to combine energy sources, in order to benefit from a share of renewable. Energy can be saved by producing electricity to heat water, generate heat. In this way, each customer can set up an optimal energy mix and save on heating costs. Heating oil, the best auxiliary energy In many situations, and especially in rural areas, heating oil remains the most attractive energy for those who do not have access to city gas. Besides renewable energies, it provides the customer with ideal energy autonomy at an attractive price. Choosing a high-efficiency heating oil, like TOTAL Mazout Ultra, also helps to save energy. It guarantees optimum performance from your installation, taking care to preserve it over time. Take advantage of green electricity and natural gas Through Lampiris, TOTAL provides its customers with 100% green electricity, mainly produced on Belgian territory by local players, as well as natural gas, the cleanest fossil energy available on the market.
