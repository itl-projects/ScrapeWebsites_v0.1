Find out about Total Accueil's commitments My service station Total's commitments: service station network in Belgium On a daily basis, TOTAL Belgium is committed to offering you high-quality products and an impeccable welcome, so that each of your visits to the of one of our service stations is a success. TOTAL Belgium's network of service stations is the most dense in the country. In each of these 500 establishments, we strive to offer you a first-class experience, through fuels, lubricants, engine cleaners and other products that protect your vehicle, but also thanks to a team at your service and a personalized welcome. A rigorous, fast and friendly service TOTAL Belgium relies on smiling employees, to always offer you a pleasant break in your day. Our dynamic service station staff are able to serve you quickly, so you don't waste time on the road. If you wish, our teams will take the time necessary to advise you thoroughly. Whether you are a regular customer or just passing through, they will pay special attention to you. Appropriately trained service station personnel Each member of the service station is trained to meet your different needs. Our employees can thus inform you about the differences between certain fuels, indicate the lubricant suitable for your vehicle or explain how you should inflate your tires. Fuels that take care of your vehicle TOTAL service stations are also premium fuels, developed from the group's research and development center, in a process of continuous improvement. Each fuel offered to the pump cleans and protects your vehicle's engine, kilometer after kilometer. TOTAL fuel also helps you reduce your fuel consumption and the environmental impact of your vehicle. Welcoming, impeccably clean service stations TOTAL Belgium aims to maintain its network at a level of excellence, particularly in terms of cleanliness, so that your visit is always pleasant. Great importance is attached to hygiene, both outside and inside gas stations, in the shop, restaurant areas, or sanitary facilities. Quality equipment To ensure that each visit to your TOTAL service stations meets your expectations, we equip them with the necessary equipment and facilities. For example, you will find gloves and paper for fueling yourself, a squeegee and water to clean your windshield while refueling your vehicle, or a device to measure the pressure of your tires and adjust it if necessary. In many stations, you can also take advantage of our shop, our restaurant or our toilets to take a break. In order to preserve and further strengthen the quality of the services offered within TOTAL service stations, these are subject to rigorous controls each year. They are carried out improvised by neutral customers who use an evaluation grid to evaluate the different services of the station.
