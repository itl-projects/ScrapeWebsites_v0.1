Home Professionals Lubricants Industry lubricants Your industry Lubricant for the materials industry Lubricants for the materials industry TOTAL Lubricants has developed a complete range of mold release products that meet the requirements of manufacturing construction materials: Technical efficiency: easy mold release, quality of the surface appearance and cleanliness of the molds Economic efficiency: good performance / cost ratio of lubrication Respect for the health of operators: non-toxic lubricants based on vegetable oil Environmental protection: our whole or soluble oils for concrete, plaster, fiber cement and clay are available in a biodegradable version. Our quality products and our high level technical assistance will help you optimize your manufacturing processes. Discover BIOMOLDOL, our range of lubricants for demolding concrete. High performance biodegradable products: Respect for the environment with a biodegradability of more than 90% High quality finish, shiny surfaces Reduction in lubricant consumption Reduction in the costs of treating used oils Better working conditions for users (odorless products, without release of vapors, without skin irritations) Unmoulding of concrete immediate and deferred Biodegradable oils: BIOMOLDOL 4, 5, 6 and 7 Mineral oils: MOLDOL P50, MOLDOL V12 Demoulding of aerated concrete MOLDOL LW583 Demoulding of concrete tiles Biodegradable oil BIOMOLDOL 7: Optimal effectiveness in brush spraying. Very low consumption: 2.5 g per tile on average.
