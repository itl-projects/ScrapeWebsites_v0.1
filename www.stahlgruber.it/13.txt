THE STAHLGRUBER GESELLSCHAFTER-STIFTUNG The shareholders of the company STAHLGRUBER founded the STAHLGRUBER GESELLSCHAFTER-STIFTUNG in 2003. To date, the shareholders have already contributed around EUR 16 million to this foundation. With the certificate of recognition from the government of Upper Bavaria, the foundation was recognized in November 2003 as a public foundation under civil law based in Poing. The purpose of the foundation is to promote technical and professional further training, in particular in the automotive and vulcanising trades, in industrial rubber technology and in related areas. The courses take place in the modern training rooms of the STAHLGRUBER GESELLSCHAFTER-STIFTUNG in Anröchte, Gröbenzell near Munich, Teutschenthal near Halle and Würzburg as well as at mobile locations.
