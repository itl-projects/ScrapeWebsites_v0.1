Over 1,100 companies have now joined one of the three specialist retail concepts on offer and receive professional support in the areas of sales, e-commerce, marketing, further education, inventory management and corporate management. At the same time, GDHS is the number one marketer of the Goodyear Dunlop Group and is consistently expanding this position. The experience and know-how of Goodyear Dunlop trading systems also serve the Goodyear Group as "best practice" for cooperation concepts with tire dealers in over 32 countries in Europe, the Middle East and Africa.
