The operation of your own online shop is a matter of course for every quick partner. Both franchisees and investors are very welcome in the "Quick family". Prerequisites for the quick partnership commercial training (automotive industry is an advantage) and sales talent suitable location in a conurbation with at least 200,000 inhabitants frequented location 500 - 800 sqm building area or building plot of 1,500 - 2,000 sqm.
