For many companies, car service has become a matter of course in the concept. The partners receive the support of our experts in the workshop equipment, staff training and know-how and processes in the workshop. You are guaranteed not to miss any regulations and you will know about the latest developments in automobile manufacturers and vehicle technology at an early stage. In addition, the GDHS successfully supports dealers in marketing high-quality rims and accessories as well as vehicle refinement (tuning).
