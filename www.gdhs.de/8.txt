With Goodyear Dunlop, as our partner, you have a guarantor for high-quality products that always has the future of the tire business and mobility in mind. Professional tire service, like trading in high-quality products, is part of our partners' main business. The professional fitting of all types of tires such as UHP and run-flat tires is a matter of course and confirmed by the wdk certificate. The GDHS partners are above all experts for tires and tire service of all kinds - from car tires to light truck to truck and commercial vehicle tires, the partners have positioned themselves depending on the business model. From tire repair to know-how relating to the installation of tire pressure monitoring systems to breakdown service, the GDHS companies work with qualified personnel and offer the most in-house service of the tire trade. The GDHS supports its partners in the marketing of truck and commercial vehicle tires with innovative and individual marketing concepts and strategies. Always up-to-date, the GDHS is at its partners' side to meet the constantly changing demands that the market and the customer have in common.
