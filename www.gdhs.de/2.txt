The one to two-day conferences are divided into lectures in the plenum, at which the participants are informed about the latest developments within the GDHS and the industry, and workshops. Here, not only is information provided on current issues, but also lively discussions. At the end of the first day of the conference, all participants meet at the evening event, where contacts are cultivated while eating together. The spring and autumn conference of the Quick Group The partners of Quick Reifendiscount also meet twice a year for a two-day conference at different locations in Germany. Together with employees of the GDHS headquarters, this will be used to discuss the current market and competitive situation and to provide information about the latest news from the industry.
