With 1,200 service stations in Germany, Austria and Switzerland and more than 4,000 partners across Europe, it is one of the leading service companies in the field of tire management. Despite the increasing price war and ever tougher competition in the fleet business, the 4Fleet Group can assert itself in the market. Well-known leasing companies, including many international companies, entrust their fleet management to the 4Fleet Group. In addition to the support of vehicle fleets in the area of ​​tires and wheels of cars, vans or commercial vehicles (trucks, light trucks), the offers also include individual agreements in the area of ​​car service, fleet-specific analyzes, professional advice on how to exploit potential for cost reduction, driver's license control and UVV tests. The 4Fleet Group thus serves as an important service component and customer supply tool for our GDHS partners and affiliated tire dealers. The contracts with the affiliated GDHS partners are processed and settled online via the European fleet accounting program "Fleet Online Solution" (FOS). Online authorization, electronic error checking for order entry and the interface to the tiresoft 3 ERP system guarantee that the 4Fleet Group partners can process orders quickly and smoothly.
