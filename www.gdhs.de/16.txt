He is consulted on all strategic questions, on decisions relevant to expansion and on aspects relating to the development of the individual trading systems. The advisory board is thus an active initiator for future processes within the GDHS network. However, its most important role is that of the mediator: the advisory board represents the interests and contacts of the dealers and arbitrators in the event of controversial questions.
