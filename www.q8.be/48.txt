CNG, Compressed Natural Gas, or pressurized natural gas is a very environmentally friendly fuel because it emits 20 to 25% less CO2 and particulate matter compared to diesel and petrol. This fuel guarantees great driving comfort and autonomy comparable to that of other fuels. With a full tank you can drive no less than 500km. With a hybrid engine that combines natural gas and petrol, that even increases to 1000km. In addition, the CNG technology is extremely safe. You can refuel and park anywhere, including underground. Do you have a car on CNG? Then you are at the right place at Q8! Q8 already opened 5 CNG filling stations in 2016, and we plan more in the future.
