Tire storage In order to optimize the service life of your tires, we store them professionally for you: cool, protected from light and in a dry and clean place. And you also save yourself the annoying haulage. Washing the wheels Brake dust and road salt can often lead to damage to the paintwork and corrosion of the wheels over time. Therefore tires and wheels should be cleaned regularly, especially before they are put into storage. We clean your tires and wheels professionally and environmentally friendly, so that you can enjoy them for a long time. Waste tire disposal Waste tires cannot simply be disposed of with household waste because they belong to special waste. We dispose of the old tires for you in an environmentally friendly and cost-effective manner, taking into account all legal provisions.
