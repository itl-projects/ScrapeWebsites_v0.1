Service of trucks of all brands and semi-trailers. Erem Serwis s.c. has extensive service experience in the field of truck and semi-trailer repairs. The plant has been on the Polish market since 1997, hence the extensive service experience in the field of truck repairs. Skills acquired in this long period pay off today. In addition to the knowledge and specialized equipment the company has two service halls with a total area of ​​over 1000 m². This makes it possible to repair trucks of all brands and semi-trailers, as well as comprehensive servicing of mechanical, electrical and pneumatic systems. Locksmith and turning facilities make it possible to carry out ongoing repairs and unusual vehicle modifications. Our factory also specializes in accident repair of trucks and trailers.
