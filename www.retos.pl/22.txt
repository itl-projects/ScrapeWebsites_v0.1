Other news Renault Trucks won the VR "Enviromental Care" award from the German magazine Verkehrs Rundschau for a range of 100% electric vehicles (ZE). VR Awards, awarded by the magazine Verkehrs Rundschau, honor truck manufacturers, transport and logistics specialists who have distinguished themselves in the field of environmental protection, technology development and training. During the second edition of the VR Awards, which took place in February 2020. In Munich, Renault Trucks received the VR "Enviromental Care" award in the truck manufacturer category for introducing the full range of electric vehicles. Managing Director Renault Trucks Germany - Frédéric Ruesche noted: "Renault Trucks is strongly focused on the development of electric vehicles using the years of experience gathered in this sector. We are proud that our electric vehicles, currently in series production, contribute to improving the quality of life in city centers. "With a complete range of electric vehicles available from 3.1 to 26 tonnes DMC, Renault Trucks meets both the current and future requirements of transport companies operating in cities. These vehicles are ideally suited to the needs of the municipal industry, enabling noiseless service at unconventional hours, while contributing to improving the quality of life among residents. Renault Trucks D and D Wide Z.E. vehicles are produced at the production plant in Blainville-sur-Orne (Calvados, France).
