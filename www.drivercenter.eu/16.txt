Dear customers, all DRIVER CENTERS throughout Germany are still open to you. Due to the current high number of incoming phone calls, we cannot accept every call personally. If you cannot reach us by phone, please use our convenient online appointment planner or send us an email. This is how we as a service provider can ensure your mobility! We are there for you and take your orders as usual. Nevertheless, we take the situation seriously and have made arrangements. Our employees are trained to implement all the required hygiene standards and adhere to the following rule: 1. Regular and careful hand washing 2. Frequently touched surfaces are cleaned or disinfected regularly 3. Our sales and workshop rooms are regularly well ventilated. We ask for your understanding for any delays in the agreed service dates. Since the situation can change daily, we ask for your help in finding the most flexible date possible. If you notice cold symptoms in yourself, please do not visit our branch personally. Ask someone else to hand over or pick up your vehicle. Thank you and stay healthy!
