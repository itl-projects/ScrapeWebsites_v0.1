From the installation of the boxes to the exploitation of the data of your trailers, you will appreciate the simplicity and the efficiency of this service. How does Findit "Where's my trailer?", The trailer location service offered by AS 24, work? 1. Install a tracker on your trailer: this box without connections installs in less than 5 minutes. 2. Install a tag on the tractor if you wish to benefit from alerts in the event of the trailer stalling. 3. Declare your boxes in the portal dedicated to monitoring your trailers. 4. Track your trailers on the portal or via email alerts. A simple and effective technology to monitor your trailers AS 24 relies on the Sigfox network to allow you to easily locate your trailers through connected objects. These autonomous boxes, operating on battery, have many advantages: They are simple and quick to install on your trailers because they do not require connections: 5 minutes are enough! Their installation does not require any specific qualification: no installation costs. They are compatible with all types of rolling stock: trailer, semi-trailer , swap body… whatever their brand. The autonomy of these boxes is 5 years. Tracking your trailers in the blink of an eyeWith "Where's my trailer?" you benefit from a secure portal dedicated to the management of your trailer fleet.
