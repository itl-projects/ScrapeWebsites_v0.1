This environmental approach is part of the government's system of Energy Savings Certificates (CEE). It is one of the flagship instruments of French policy to control energy consumption. The Energy Savings Certificate system forces energy “sellers” to develop a certain amount of action to save energy. Transporters are directly affected by the EWC scheme. AS 24 is your privileged partner and helps you achieve energy savings! The positive effects of EWCs for your transport company AS 24 is committed to helping you in your energy transition by paying for your energy saving actions: AS 24 pays you a premium which is determined based on the actions you undertake and which have been validated by the Administration. At the same time, controlling your energy consumption allows you to reduce your operating costs. Finally, you value your image with your customers or partners.
