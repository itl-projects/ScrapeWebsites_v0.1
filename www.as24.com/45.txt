Viabox is the name of the badge used to collect the tax. Warning ! This system does not integrate all of the country's toll roads. Some motorways are still managed by private operators, such as the A2 between Warsaw and Berlin. The toll then concerns all motorized vehicles with a total authorized load weight greater than 3.5 tonnes and buses with more than 9 seats. Your AS 24 toll solutions in Poland: how it works The applicable toll systems In Poland, the toll payment (only Viatoll) is fully automatic thanks to the On Board Unit (OBU) system, which works by microwave . Road users entering Poland are required by law to take a badge, Pre- or Post-Pay. AS 24Post-Pay solutions With the Post-Pay service, you benefit from a deferral on your next AS 24 invoice. Traffic on the A2 Świecko (German border) - Kukuryki (Belarusian border) is done at the toll kiosks thanks to the EUROTRAFIC card. Product AS 24 EUROTRAFIC card Materialization Description The card which gives you access to the toll and station networks in Europe. It also facilitates the payment of foreign taxes. Operation at the toll station Portals are built on the road network subject to toll. They are equipped with antennas ensuring communications with the On Board Unit Viatoll. When passing under a toll gate, an audible signal emitted by the On Board Unit indicates to the driver that the toll has been recorded. The Polish system is mixed, with gantry cranes and toll stations.
