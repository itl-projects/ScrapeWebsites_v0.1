This offer allows automatic inspection of tires when heavy goods vehicles pass through AS 24 é service stations equipped with the device *. The objective of this service, marketed by AS 24, is to optimize the maintenance of tires thanks to automated controls of inflation pressure and tire wear to use them under always optimal conditions: guarantee of safety on the road and saving natural resources. The inspection does not require any action on the part of the driver or the maintenance manager. The main benefits of Fleet Diag 24: Safety and serenityRespect of regulationsOptimization of tire maintenanceFuel control better Integrated in the Fleet Diag 24 offer, MICHELIN QuickScan is a simple and efficient system for more sustainable performance. It works in all weathers. More than 15 Michelin patents were necessary for its design. Exclusively designed by Michelin, the system works for any type of vehicle (tractor, trailer, carrier) and for any brand of truck tire. The MICHELIN QuickScan systemThis reliable and discreet measurement tool allows: Automated tire wear monitoring carried out using magnetic scanners. It works whatever the weather conditions (rain, snow etc.). Tire pressure measurement performed using a radio frequency receiver coupled to the scanner (functionality available in the fall of 2020). A kit is available, consisting of: An RFID tag ** to be affixed to the chassis of the vehicle allowing its identification. Valve caps screwed onto the wheels (available in the fall of 2020) to know the tire pressure. In addition to this system, a series of diagnostic tools focused on user needs is offered to truck fleets. It makes it possible to obtain diagnostic reports co-designed with fleets (state of tire wear, under-inflation detected, number of vehicles not seen, etc.), and to suggest suggestions for relevant actions delivered by Michelin experts. (ex: swapping on rim, tire maintenance to be planned quickly…) Pascal Zammit, director of the connected mobility entity at Michelin: “This offer is fully in line with Michelin's vision of All Sustainable which promotes the emergence of connected solutions and innovative to encourage safer, more efficient, more sustainable and environmentally friendly mobility. This partnership with AS 24 is a great opportunity to make our innovation accessible to a large number of fleets thanks to the strength of its network. Manuel Olivier, President AS 24: “AS 24 is delighted to offer the Fleet Diag 24 solution to transport professionals, in partnership with Michelin. We optimize the time spent by our customers in our service stations by allowing them to receive a diagnosis of the tires in their fleet for more safety.
