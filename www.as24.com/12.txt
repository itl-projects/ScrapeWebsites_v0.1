The Eurovignette is the tax paid by road users. Trucks with a total permissible laden weight of at least 12 tonnes must be in possession of the Eurovignette to use Danish motorways. Your AS 24 toll solutions in Denmark: how it works? Applicable toll systems It is possible to '' buy a sticker for the desired period (by the day, by the month, etc.). For this purpose, you must go through an AGES terminal and enter the vehicle registration number, the number of axles, etc. Then , you make the transaction via the EUROTRAFIC card. A receipt is issued: it allows you to pass the toll station (no document like a "real" sticker will be issued). The information is centralized in a database for each Member State.
