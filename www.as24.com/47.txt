Optimize your cash flow with AS 24 and EUROVAT, you recover your VAT and diesel taxes more simply, throughout Europe. Who is EuroVAT? EUROVAT is a specialized company in tax recovery, expert in its field for more than 20 years. How do you get your VAT back? 1) You are an AS 24 customer.2) EUROVAT receives your invoices, creates and sends your files to the tax authorities. 3) The tax services process your files and reimburse the VAT. 4) Upon receipt EUROVAT refunds the VAT to you.
