Your AS 24 toll solutions in Italy: how it works The Viacard solution Mainly used for occasional trips in Italy, the Viacard allows you to cross tolls at all Italian stations. A Viacard is interchangeable: it can be used by any truck in your fleet. The Telepass solution In the event that you regularly use Italian motorways, this telebadge system allows the automatic opening of toll gates, without stopping therefore without any loss of time. You take advantage of the special queues which avoid traffic jams at the toll booths. Each truck has its own Telepass. With AS 24, no more administrative paperwork!
