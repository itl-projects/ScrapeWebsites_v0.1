The Belgian Kilometric Tax is an intelligent kilometer charge system. It concerns heavy goods vehicles over 3.5 tonnes (total authorized weight loaded> 3.5 Tons) Belgian and foreigners traveling on motorways and certain regional or municipal roads in the 3 regions of Belgium. Your AS 24 toll solutions in Belgium: how it works Since April 1, 2016, a new toll system has been applied in Belgium. Each truck over 3.5 tonnes must be equipped with a satellite box called OBU (On Board Unit). It replaces the Eurovignette in Belgium for trucks over 12 tonnes. The OBU records the kilometers and records the routes taken by your trucks. This new system applies to Belgian motorways and certain major axes of the country, covering a total of more than 6,800 km. Discover the detailed taxable network and download the maps of the regions on the Viapass website. The prices (€ / km) vary according to four criteria: type of route class of emission kilometers traveled card that gives you access to the toll networks and stations in Europe.
