The goal ? Responding very precisely to the needs of truck transport professionals. CardControl AS 24 is the ideal tool which allows fleet managers to set spending limits and manage the activation of their fuel and toll cards in circulation on their fleet. Fix blocking ceilings thanks to CardControl With the CardControl solution, you yourself fix ceilings to your AS 24 fuel cards, according to the needs of your drivers. This therefore ensures you a maximum level of security on all of the transactions carried out by your fleet at a service station. To guarantee better control over your current needs as well as the evolution of your fuel expenses, CardControl is inevitably a precious help! CardControl: control your expenses in just a few clicks concerning: the acquisition of fuels in L / calendar day and / or up to 31 sliding days (up to 3 possible ceilings, all cumulative products: GO, FOD, GNR and AdBlue®). unauthorized collection days or times of day unauthorized or prohibited countries or stations (maximum 50). You can modify the limits and restrictions of AS 24 fuel cards as needed, without having to request the creation of new cards. Manage the activation of your AS 24 cards via CardControl Thanks to CardControl, you can suspend the use of your cards for an indefinite period from your Customer Area. A suspended card cannot be used on the AS 24 network. You decide to reactivate the card as soon as you want. The suspend / activate functionality of your AS 24 cards is very useful in the case where: one of your vehicles is immobilized for repair. one of your drivers is arrested for an indefinite period. one of your cards is lost or stolen. you wish to have more control over the use of your cards.
