Toll Collect service We are an authorized service partner of the Toll Collect GmbH company, which is the operator of the highway toll system in Germany. Our authorization has been confirmed by her with relevant training and certification. Users who want to use the automatic toll collection on toll road sections in Germany must register their company with the operator Toll Collect. The appropriate registration form for a new Toll Collect user can be downloaded and printed from www.toll-collect.de or ordered by phone at +49 (0) 180 2865526. Our authorized Toll Collect service offers, among others, assembly and disassembly of devices Toll Collect in all truck brands, update of Toll Collect devices, replacement of Toll Collect devices, change of truck data in the automatic toll collection system.
