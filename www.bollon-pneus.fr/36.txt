Excellent hydroplanning properties Rolls easily and thus consumes little fuel. The ideal tire for the passenger car The ZEON CS6 from COOPER is a summer tire intended for passenger vehicles. The design of these asymmetrical and grooved sculptures ensures regular wear, comfortable and silent driving. This tire offers excellent resistance to aquaplaning by effectively wicking water under the tread.The new rubber compound in the tread improves grip, braking on wet roads and increases tire life . This lighter tire than its predecessor offers precise handling and low rolling resistance. With rims, it protects the rim. About COOPER The manufacturer COOPER has been in existence since 1914 and has grown very quickly. The headquarters are in Findlay, Ohio and COOPER is present in more than 60 countries. Very popular with American consumers, it is the second largest manufacturer in the United States. The European headquarters are in Melksham in the United Kingdom and subsidiaries are present in France, Germany, Italy, Spain and Switzerland. Only one thing counts for COOPER: staying at the forefront. Innovation remains the engine of the efficiency of our process. Cooper offers a full range of tires to meet the needs of all consumers and meet the demands of global markets. The COOPER brand is particularly specialized in tires for 4x4 vehicles.
