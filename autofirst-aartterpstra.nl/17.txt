All-season tires are a compromise between summer and winter tires. Thanks to the special rubber compound and profile construction, they provide good performance in all weather conditions. You can use it all year round, without changing tires. If you do not drive too often in winter conditions and cover less than 10,000 kilometers per year, these tires are an excellent choice. Are all-season tires approved as a winter tire? Almost all new all-season tires are approved as a winter tire. If you drive with older tires, check carefully whether there is a snowflake on the sidewall of your tires. In that case, they are approved and also meet the strict rules that apply in Germany, for example. For a wide range of tires?
