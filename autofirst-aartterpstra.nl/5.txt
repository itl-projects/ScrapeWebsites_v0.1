Since our establishment in 2002, we perform all activities in the field of maintenance, repairs, damage and MOT inspections. We do not only focus on the private market, but we are also specialized in commercial vehicles. With our fast service and competitive prices, we can already count a large number of companies, with an extensive fleet, among our customers. We supply and assemble for all brands of car parts such as Tires, Batteries, Exhausts, Towbars etc. at competitive prices. In our occasion corner you will find a range of selectively purchased cars in excellent maintenance condition. All our cars are delivered with a new MOT inspection. We have a workshop with the most modern equipment. We have 4 bridges available for our work and our well-trained and experienced technicians specialize in all makes of cars. Repair and maintenance of air conditioning installation are also part of our field. In our garage, we find direct and personal contact between the customer and mechanic very important. A characteristic of our company is therefore high customer-friendliness, service and craftsmanship. We specialize in service and maintenance services according to factory standards.
