Using the timing belt, the crankshaft of your car drives the camshaft (s). Sometimes a diesel injection pump is also driven at the same time. The toothed belt is made of strong rubber and plastic, reinforced with wires. What is the difference between a timing chain and belt? When should the timing belt be replaced? What is a distribution chain? Your car has a timing chain or belt. Both have the same function. However, the timing chain is made of hardened steel and, unlike the timing belt, does not need to be replaced. Consult your car owner's manual to find out if there is a timing chain or belt. Of course you can also contact AutoFirst Aart Terpstra. When should the belt be replaced? Replacing a timing belt is part of regular maintenance. It is replaced preventively at a certain mileage. Your car's owner's manual or maintenance schedule will tell you when it should be done. If you drive few kilometers, the belt may dry out before it needs to be replaced according to the odometer. It is also advisable to replace it earlier for cars that make many short trips in the city. A timing belt then wears out comparatively harder. How do I check if the belt needs to be replaced? The service book of your car always clearly states the last replacement date. Usually there is also a sticker under the hood with the date and mileage on which the belt was last replaced. Can it no longer be traced? AutoFirst Aart Terpstra can indicate on the basis of your license plate when the timing belt needs to be replaced. What is the risk of a worn timing belt? If the belt is not replaced in time, it can break in the worst case. A dangerous situation, because your engine suddenly crashes. This results in serious engine damage and a repair that runs heavily in the papers. If the belt does not break but slips, your car will run badly or no longer. The repair costs are also considerable in this case. What activities are involved in replacing a timing belt? It is often wise to replace the tensioners, idlers and even the water pump at the same time as the timing belt. These wear-sensitive parts, like the timing belt, have run the necessary kilometers. If you still need to have these parts replaced at a later date, the entire repair will be carried out again. What are the costs for replacing the belt? The replacement is a major job, which can vary greatly over time depending on your car type.
