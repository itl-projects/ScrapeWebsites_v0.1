Choose our aero and nyco lubricants for the aeronautical industry Home Professionals Lubricants Lubricants industry Your industry Automotive industry Aero and nyco lubricants for the aeronautical industry Safety and aircraft performance With the AERO and NYCO Aviation ranges, TOTAL Lubricants offers a full range of lubricants for aircraft in general and civil aviation. All products meet international specifications. TOTAL Lubrifiants is a distributor of NYCO Aviation products. Discover our lubrication products: Piston engines Aircraft turbomachinery Helicopter transmissions Helicopter reactors Turbines Hydraulic systems Greases Piston engines AERO 80, AERO 100, AERO 120: Mineral oils for piston engines, when the use of dispersive oil is not recommended. AERO 80 is used for running in piston engines AERO D 80, AERO D 100, AERO D 120: Mineral dispersive grade mineral oils for piston engines of aircraft operating under severe and very severe conditions AERO DM 15W50, AERO DM 20W60 : Ashless multigrade dispersive oils for aircraft piston engines. Usable all year round, in all climates. Products approved by the FAA Aircraft turbomachinery AEROTURBINE 222, AEROTURBINE 331, AEROTURBINE 921: Mineral oils for aircraft turbomachines, certified by TURBOMECA Transmissions of helicopter AEROGEAR 823, AEROGEAR 1032: Extreme pressure mineral oils for gearboxes loaded with helicopters. Products approved by EUROCOPTER AEROTURBINE 540 helicopter reactors: Synthetic oil for aircraft turbomachines. Qualified by TURBOMECA and EUROCOPTER Turbines TURBONYCOIL 160: Synthetic oil for aircraft gas turbofan engines, but also accessories and auxiliary power units (APU) TURBONYCOIL 600: Synthetic oil for aircraft gas turbojet engines. Validated by major engine manufacturers (General Electric, Pratt and Whitney, Allison, Rolls-Royce, Allied Signal, Snecma) Hydraulic systems AEROHYDRAULIQUE 520: Mineral hydraulic fluid for systems operating under high pressures and low temperatures at very low HYDRAUNYCOIL FH 51 : Microfiltered mineral hydraulic fluid for the hydraulic circuits of aircraft and various land equipment. Also used as a shock absorbing fluid in the landing gear of civil aircraft (Airbus, ATR, Bombardier, Fokker, Embraer, etc.) HYDRAUNYCOIL FH 42: Low temperature synthetic hydraulic fluid. Can be used between - 54 ° C and + 200 ° C, for an extended period and without undergoing significant evaporation or change in viscosity. Also used on autopilot systems, brakes and shock absorbers HYDRAUNYCOIL FH 2: Micro filtered synthetic hydraulic fluid. Usable between - 40 ° C and + 160 ° C, with peak operation possibilities up to 210 ° C HYDRAUNYCOIL FH 5 AW: Mineral damping fluid, intended for the landing gear of Boeing, Mc Donnell Douglas type aircraft and Lockheed. Validated by Boeing according to the BMS 3-32 Type II specification. Should not be used for the storage of landing gear HYDRAUNYCOIL FH 6 AW: Mineral damping fluid, intended for the landing gear of aircraft of type Boeing, Mc Donnell Douglas and Lockheed. Validated by Boeing according to the BMS 3-32 Type I specification. Used as a preservative for landing gear following maintenance operations NYCOGREASE GN 10 Greases: General purpose synthetic aviation grease. Recommended for equipment driven by low power drive units. Suitable for heavily loaded bearings, gears and sliding surfaces of low power devices. Ideal for components exposed to very low temperatures down to - 73 ° C NYCOGREASE GN 17: Grease based on lithium soap and synthetic diester oil thickened with a solid lubricant (contains 5% molybdenum disulfide). Lubrication of aeronautical equipment over the temperature range from -73 ° C to + 121 ° C NYCOGREASE GN 22: Synthetic grease NLGI 2 thickened with concrete for general use. Suitable for the most severe conditions (heavy loads, high temperatures, contamination by water), especially on wheel bearings. The range of use is from -65 ° C to + 177 ° C NYCOGREASE GN 27: Synthetic grease with high load capacity containing molybdenum disulphide and operating over a temperature range from -54 ° C to + 177 ° C NYCOGREASE GN HC: Synthetic grease thickened with lithium soap, resistant to hydrocarbons, alcohols and water. Intended for the lubrication of valves, fuel circuits, or "methanol-water". Also used on valves conveying hot crude oil or natural gas in the petroleum industry NYCOGREASE GN GA 47: Graphite anti-seize product composed of a mixture of petroleum jelly and micronized graphite. Used for threaded elements exposed to high temperatures.
