TOTAL Mazout Ultra heating oil of superior quality Home My house Heating with oil Heating oil of superior quality: Total Mazout Ultra Thanks to its unique composition, Total Mazout Ultra allows you to consume and pollute less, but also to keep your heating system in better condition. You need to place an order for fuel oil but you don't know what is the difference between regular heating oil and Ultra Fuel Oil? We answer all your questions in the following lines. What is Total Mazout Ultra, our high efficiency fuel oil? Total Mazout Ultra heating oil is distinguished from ordinary heating oil by its composition. It contains a series of additives that provide you with many benefits, including anti-corrosion agents and antioxidants. In addition, Total Fuel Oil Ultra has a reduced content of sulfur (<50 ppm) and metals, which also better protects your installation and therefore allows you to save money. What are the advantages of TOTAL Mazout Ultra heating oil? Due to its composition, Total Mazout Ultra heating oil has many advantages. Total Fuel Oil Ultra protects your installation Thanks to the anti-corrosion additive it contains, it cleans and protects all the metallic components of your heating installation: tank; power circuit; pump ; filter; sprinkler; combustion chamber ; foyer. TOTAL Mazout Ultra is also more stable than regular fuel oil thanks to the antioxidant agent it contains. This helps limit the build-up of deposits in your tank, which reduces your maintenance costs. In addition, TOTAL Fuel Oil Ultra is more resistant to temperature variations. In general, it is more stable than ordinary fuel oil, whether it is: light stability; thermal stability; oxidation stability. Total Fuel Oil Ultra protects the environment Designed to improve the efficiency of your boiler, Total Fuel Oil Heating Ultra allows complete combustion which therefore clogs your heating installation less. This advantage is coupled with an environmental advantage: soot and carbon dioxide (SO2) emissions are considerably reduced, in particular due to the lower sulfur content of TOTAL Mazout Ultra. Total Fuel Oil Ultra Saves You The use of Total Fuel Oil Heating Ultra reduces the fouling of your heating system and therefore reduces the risk of breakdown and maintenance costs. In addition, its lower sulfur content also helps to reduce your consumption. Using Total Mazout Ultra therefore allows you to save money.
