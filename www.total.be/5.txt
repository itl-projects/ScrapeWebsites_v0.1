Frequently asked questions Your card and points Where can I get a Total Club card? The Total Club card is available, on request and free of charge, at all Total stations in the network in Belgium participating in the loyalty program. How do I redeem my points? You can exchange your points for happyfuel fuel cards, promotional items or pay directly for your purchases in the Total shop. How can I check the balance of my points? You can check the balance of your points by logging on to total.be or via the Total app. Your balance can also be viewed via your transaction tickets. I'm at the station and can't redeem my points? If you are unable to redeem your points at the station, your card has not been registered. You can register your card via our total.be site, in the "register your card" section. I filled up outside opening hours can I validate the ticket on my Total Club card? Yes, you have 30 days to validate the ticket on your Total Club card at the station where you refueled, based on the payment terminal ticket. I note that my point balance is at 0. If you have not used your card in the last 12 months, the balance of your points is automatically set to 0. (see article 2.2 of the Total Club regulations) I have done full and it was saved on my card but I can't find it on my account. Normally information from all stations is downloaded overnight to the central server. Depending on when you are full, your transaction will be visible the next day or after 2 days. I do not know my card number. The number of your card is indicated on the back of the card, under the barcode. Your card number starts with 9926, or 9924 for Truckers. The ticket for your transaction also mentions your card number and the balance of your points. I could not find the answer to my question. What can I do ? Please contact our Call Center at +32 2 288 33 22 and choose option 2 after choosing the language for further information. Frequently asked questions Lost or stolen card What should I do if I lose a Total Club card? If you have lost your Total Club card, you can block it in the “lost / stolen card” sub-tab of your customer account, by logging in using your old card number. You can then transfer your details from your old card to a new one. If you no longer have your old card number, contact the Call Center at +32 2 288 33 22 (option 2 after choosing the language) Can I recover the points from a lost or stolen card? Yes, you can transfer all your points and contact details from your old card to a new card by logging in using your old card number. If you no longer have it, contact the Call Center at +32 2 288 33 22 (option 2 after choosing the language) How do I block my Total Club card? You can block your card in your Total Club account on the "lost / stolen card" section. Just log in with your old card number. If you no longer have it, contact the Call Center at +32 2 288 33 22 (option 2 after choosing the language) What can you do to get a new card? We invite you to look for a new card at a Total station.
