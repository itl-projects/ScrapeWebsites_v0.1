Discover our lubricants for the steel industry Home Professionals Lubricants Lubricants for industry Your industry Lubricants for the steel industry Efficient lubrication solutions for the steel industry Thanks to its experience in industrial processes in the steel industry, TOTAL has consolidated its reputation as a specialist in your market. We provide our customers with high performance lubrication solutions, for all your applications, and which meet your expectations: Machine availability and productivity Reliability of the manufacturing process Energy savings Reduction of carbon emissions With a presence in more than With 160 countries and a full range of lubrication products and services, TOTAL Lubricants is your partner supplier for all your installations around the world. Greases for agglomeration, continuous casting, cold rolling, hot rolling CERAN range: High-tech greases with complex calcium sulfonate, developed by TOTAL for many years. The manufacturing process for these greases requires real expertise to achieve the exceptional level of performance of CERANs. Outstanding anti-wear properties and resistance to load Exceptional water resistance allowing operation in the presence of water up to contents of up to 40% water without altering the performance of the grease Excellent mechanical stability and shear resistance high Good behavior at low temperatures Very good resistance to oxidation and corrosion Hydrodynamic lubrication oils for regulating bearings MORGOIL, MORGAN CORTIS MS: Multipurpose anti-wear circulation oil, particularly indicated when there are risks of pollution by some water. It meets the lubrication requirements of DANIELI and MORGOIL type bearings used in rolling mills in the steel industry. Excellent resistance to oxidation and thermal stability Extension of the service life of the lubricant in service even subject to high temperatures Remarkable demulsion properties validated by high and low temperature tests and confirmed by the UEC dynamic demulsion test. Very good anti-wear properties Optimal protection of equipment against rust and corrosion at high temperature Ashless additive limiting the formation of deposits and allowing good filterability to be maintained Fire-resistant hydraulic fluids HYDRANSAFE range: Hydraulic fluids specifically designed to limit risks propagation of fire in the event of a leak. Excellent filterability and oxidation resistance performance Factory Mutual Approvals Meets ISO HFAE, HFAS, HFC, HFDU, HFDR specifications Biodegradable version: HYDRANSAFE HFDU offers ultimate biodegradability (according to OECD 301B standard) Gasometer sealing oil GASO AW: Protects metallic surfaces subjected to the action of water, dust and corrosive fumes. Excellent demulsion and sealing properties. Dielectric fluids for EDT (Electric Discharge Texturing) machines DIEL MS: Very good filterability. Reduction of fluid consumption. Product complies with European directives concerning the emission of VOCs (Volatile Organic Compounds). CARTER ALS industrial gear oils: Non-staining reducing oil, recommended for lubrication of enclosed gears, in the aluminum rolling, stainless steel and copper rolling industries. Excellent extreme pressure and anti-wear properties ensuring good protection of lubricated members Very good resistance to oxidation, for a longer service life of the oil in service Very good resistance to foaming and to the formation of emulsion with water KASSILA GMP: Mineral oil for industrial gears operating under very high load and repeated shock. Contains graphite and molybdenum disulfide. Excellent thermal stability. Reinforced protection against corrosion and micropitting. FINAROL prelubes: Specifically developed for the steel industry and automotive construction. Dedicated to the protection and mild deformation of steel sheets and electro-galvanized steels. OSYRIS protective oils: Oils for all metals, intended to protect parts from the risk of corrosion during storage and transport. Apply by soaking, spraying and brushing, and can be removed with solvents and detergents. We offer a wide variety of protective oils depending on the desired characteristics of the protective film. Nature and thickness of the film: oily, waxy or solvent-based. Resistance over time: interoperative protection with water-repellent effect or longer-term protection FINASOL solvents: Degreasers used to clean engines and parts of very diverse materials, without altering the quality of the elements cleaned. The tailor-made recommendation We recommend that you contact us for a recommendation that is perfectly suited to your operating conditions.
