"Parking Platanos" operates with a self-service system (without the intervention of the staff of the Station), while special places have been provided for the service of the citizens with reduced mobility (disabled spaces). In addition, it has special dimensions for large cars. It also has a waiting area for passengers, while access can be made either from the stairwells or from elevators located in the beautiful square that has been formed in the above-ground space. In addition, double entry-exit ramps have been provided so that two cars can be serviced simultaneously. The characteristics of the Station that make the difference: Total Area of ​​space: 16,143 sq.m. More than 600 seats on 6 levels to always find a place immediately (STD, VIP, disabled). Double entry-exit ramps for entry and exit without delays. Wide sloping ramps from one level to another for easy access. Positions for all needs: for large cars, for two-wheelers, for the disabled. Self-service (automatic input-output bars, automatic payment machines) Advanced, automated card system for immediate service. (3) stairways & (2) elevators Possibility of entry-exit 24 hours a day.
