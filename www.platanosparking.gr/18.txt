Last update 14/12/2017 Platanos Prepaid Card Get flexibility and freedom of movement with the Platanos Prepaid Card. Save time at a discounted price! The Platanos Prepaid Card provides you with 24-hour access to the Station and use one of the available parking spaces1. To access the Station, simply "show" your Card to the input / output machines (close to the relevant marking :) This saves time since you do not have to pay at the cash register or automatic payment machines every time you use the Station! By using the Card you save not only time but also money, since: - each hour of parking is charged with an amount of € 1.75, which is deducted from the balance of the Card, - the maximum daily amount of charge is € 7 per visit / use Station. You can pay the amount you want to charge for parking time on your Card. The Card is recharged and therefore you can at any time add money to the Card to obtain additional parking time according to the above, of your choice. The available balance of your card is displayed on the screen of the input machine each time you enter the Station with your Card. You can also be informed about the use and the rest of your Card at the Central Fund of the Station. The minimum initial charge is thirty-five euros (€ 35) 2. The Card is valid for one year from the date of initial charging, and is recharged at any time with a minimum amount of six euros (€ 6). Also, each time it is recharged with a minimum amount of ten to four euros (€ 14) and above, it is renewed for one year from the date of recharging. So, in practice, the card may never expire. The Card is used: - as an Access Card (Input - Output) for the car declared in Card3, - at any time, if it contains at least the minimum cash balance of € 1.75 for parking a minimum time of 1 hour, throughout the period of validity of. In case the balance of your Card is exhausted when you leave the Station, you come to the Central Fund of the Station and recharge it. 1 out of position VIP 2 After 14 days from the date of issue of the Card, the unused balance of the Card is agreed non-refundable and can be consumed only for the use of the Station during the validity of the card.
