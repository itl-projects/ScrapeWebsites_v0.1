The solution to the parking problem of Kifissia parked forever under the Platanos square; In the center of Kifisia, under a nice green park. There, a new, large, modern 6 levels-underground parking awaits. One of the best parking stations in Europe which contributes to improving the quality of life of residents and visitors of Kifissia, with excellent value for money for both hourly and monthly parking. The parking is designed with high security standards, with amenities and with the latest technology systems for fast service.  All this, to park at a glance; Parking becomes a pleasant experience. At the parking station you will shortly find all our services that we have planned for you, so that you can take best advantage of your time (Car Wash, drop off Dry Cleaning, fellow passengers waiting area, EV charging station, bike sharing and more).
