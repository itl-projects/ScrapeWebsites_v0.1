THE COMPANY MASPROFIL-HISTORICAL COMMERCIAL DYNAMIC-ECONOMIC OBJECTIVES - ANNOUNCEMENTS News - Announcements 22/5/2017 12:00 PM exhibition of contemporary art "NECESSITY", May 22, 2017, at the facilities of Platanos Parking. Opening: Monday 22 May 2017, Time 19.30 Duration: 22 May - 10 June Monday - Sunday: 17:30 - 21:30 The needs of people and the needs of cities, artists and art lovers are redefined in a special exhibition that will be hosted at facilities of Platanos Parking, one of the most modern in Europe. The Weedschat team curates and presents works by important artists from the fields of painting, sculpture, urban art, installations and 3D mapping. With this report, the WC wants to remind, once again, that all forms of visual expression follow only the rules of their creators. No restraint restrains the artist except the one the artist himself has chosen for himself.
