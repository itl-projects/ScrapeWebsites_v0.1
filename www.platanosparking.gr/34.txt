Platanos Prepaid Card Get flexibility and freedom of movement at a discounted price! CONTINUOUS QUESTIONS What is the opening hours of the Station? The station is open to the public 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Service for passing customers is provided daily at all hours. How and when do I pay if I occasionally use the Station? At the entrance you receive your ticket, pressing the unique button on the relevant 'ticket entry / issuance device', with the date and time of your entry at the Station, which you hold until you leave the Station in your car. Park your car and leave the nearest elevator or stairwell. You pay when you return to pick up your car: - at the checkout of the Station or - at the automatic payment machines in the 1st basement and at the entrance-exit of the Station near the road. To enter, enter your ticket at the entrance customer identification device to open the entrance door to the stairwell or enter your ticket at the relevant reception in the elevator to use it. To pay for the automatic machines: - enter the ticket in the corresponding slot, - see the amount due on the screen, - pay in coins or banknotes of 5, 10, 20 euros from the corresponding slot and - Receive your ticket, the receipt of your payment, as well as any leftovers from the 'coin return case' at the bottom of the machine. At the exit, on your left, from the driver's seat, enter the ticket in the relevant socket of the output device, so that the exit bar rises and you leave driving. In case you need any help, you can press the 'intercom request' button on the right, both on the 'ticket entry / exit' device and on the 'exit device', as well as on the customer identification devices at its entrances.
