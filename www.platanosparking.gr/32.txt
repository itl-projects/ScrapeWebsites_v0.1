THE COMPANY MASPROFIL-HISTORICAL COMMERCIAL DYNAMIC-ECONOMIC ELEMENTS - ANNOUNCEMENTS News - Announcements 10/3/2015 12/10/2015 at 15:00 in Athens for the Best Bachelor in Europe) Platanos PARKING (short list in the default list Platanos PARKING, the new underground car station of the GEK TERNA Group in Kifissia, qualified for the three best parking lots in Europe in the "New Car Stations" category, as part of the "European Parking Awards" organized by the European Parking Federation (European Federation of Hotels). Association). The winner will be announced at the Federation's conference on September 23, 2015, in Berlin. The Platanos PARKING Car Station started its operation in September 2014, in order to provide a solution to the chronic parking problem faced by the area of ​​Kifissia. The multi-storey car park with a total capacity of 617 seats is a space of high technology and aesthetics, which serves the residents, visitors of the city, employees and those who use the electric railway, offering special discount packages for each use. The Underground Station operates with a self-service system, while special places have been created for the service of people with reduced mobility. In addition, it has a waiting area for passengers, areas for charging electric cars, special dimensions for large cars and two-wheelers, while there is also a car wash.
