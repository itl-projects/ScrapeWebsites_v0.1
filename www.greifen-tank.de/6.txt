JMP battery monitor 2.0. | Greifen Tank GmbH Greifen Tank GmbH 038328-70745 welcome 11.05.2020 There is hardly anything more annoying than a striking battery for motorcyclists and motorists, with the JMP battery monitor this annoyance is a thing of the past. With the battery monitor from JMP, users can keep an eye on the state of charge at all times. The eyelets are connected directly to the battery. The device then measures the voltage and the user can call up the results via an app with the JMP battery monitor 2.0. complete start-up processes and charging functions of the alternator can also be checked. Once connected, the tool measures and records the voltage, and reports can be exported as an Excel file. In the event of voltage problems, there is a corresponding notification directly on the smartphone.
