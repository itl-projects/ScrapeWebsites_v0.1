The Blue Elephant token is the guarantee of being able to wash your car in one of our 470 centers throughout France! Value of a token = 2 € The advantage of tokens is to benefit from decreasing prices for your washing, knowing that the nominal value of a token remains of 2 €. By buying ten tokens, you benefit from discounts and have a reserve to leave in the car. As soon as you have 10 minutes available, you can go to a station that crosses your path. You can directly go to a high pressure washing track or to the roller, without going through the distributor box. Control boxes on the washing tracks Our control boxes, on the high pressure washing tracks and on the washing payment terminal automatic roller, also accept coins from (1 € and 2 €). Thanks to the various means of payment available at the Blue Elephant car wash stations, you are 100% free to choose the one that suits you best according to your uses and needs.
