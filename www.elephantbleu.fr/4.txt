Proximity, freedom, security ElEphant Bleu, a leading local network for washing With nearly 470 washing stations spread throughout France and installed in commercial areas or highways, you will inevitably come across a Blue Elephant on your way, near you, near your place of work or your vacation. Eléphant Bleu is the leading car wash network in France., present everywhere. A Blue Elephant station will inevitably cross your path! When you want, in self-service or with assistance, whatever the weather and as you want, washing with jets or with the roller, rapid washing or large cleaning, exterior and interior, you control the washing mode, the duration and the budget you wish to devote to it… Depending on the stations, you have the choice of washing mode, with High Pressure jets or automatic gantry, always respecting your bodywork. Additional offers are available, such as the rim washer on the track or the space dedicated to cleaning the interior of your car. Did you know that the washing tracks can also accommodate motorcycles and two-wheelers? The washing centers offer great flexibility in schedules: most of the brand's centers are open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Designed to provide you with ease of movement and with no shaded areas, you will feel at home. comfortable and secure in the Blue Elephant stations to pamper your car. Our automatic distributors give great freedom of choice in the means of payment: currency, universal token (valid in all Blue Elephant centers), as well as the washing key and the Pro Card.
