Otherwise, use our contact form. Are the tokens valid in all your Blue Elephant centers? The Blue Elephant tokens that you buy at our centers are valid everywhere in France at all of our Blue Elephant washing stations. Where can I find the unique number of my washing key? You will find the unique identification number of your Blue Elephant washing key on the tickets when recharging at the payment terminal. You can also request this number from the manager of your Blue Elephant washing station during opening hours. Is my Blue Elephant washing key valid at all centers? Your washing key is valid at the center where you bought it and is not valid nationally. However on certain centers your Blue Elephant key can be valid on several Blue Elephant washing centers, in this case, the list of centers is indicated to you directly near the terminal or on a ticket provided with the Blue Elephant washing key . Can you provide me with promotional items (stuffed animals, pens, gratoires, balloons ...) Blue Elephant? We do not provide advertising items from our national headquarters. Advertising items are only available during our commercial operations in our Blue Elephant centers and according to the commercial conditions in force at the center. Do you sponsor associations or athletes? At the national level, we do not sponsor associations or sportspeople. However, our franchisees (independent washing centers) can participate in local life and in this context do sports or association sponsorship. We invite you to contact your center directly usual Blue Elephant wash to let him know about your project and whether he is interested. How not to damage my rims with the rim washing product? Our rim washer is optimal for almost all rims. It is not suitable for polished, chrome or repainted rims. It is to be sprayed on dry rims with more emphasis on the front than on the rear, without spraying for too long. Once the product has been applied, first wash the bodywork and finish with the rims, to allow time for the product to d 'to act. At the end of the high pressure washing, why is my vehicle full of white marks? These white traces are due to the limestone and minerals in the water following insufficient rinsing during the "finishing" step. We invite you during your next wash to insist on the washing position "finishing" which is essential Take advantage of its anti-trace action, which removes limescale residues using demineralized water. We invite you to contact the manager of the Blue Elephant center where you made the purchase, with your purchase ticket. The telephone number is available directly on the center (independent shopping sign) or on our website on the presentation page of your washing station. You will find a contact form and telephone number. Our washing center manager will verify your complaint with you using your purchase ticket and the cameras.
