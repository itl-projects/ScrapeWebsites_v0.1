Discover the advantages of the card PRO ÉLÉPHANT BLEUWith the Blue Elephant Pro Card, your employees (technicians, salespeople, managers, etc.) will be able to access the services of our very extensive network of washing stations. This Card is used as a means of payment and allows You to buy washes at the automatic gantry via its bank terminal From the station's distributor: To buy washing tokens and reload a Blue Elephant washing key To buy a Blue Elephant wash according to available credit. Controlled budget Cards are credited and reloadable automatically according to your instructions. Reloading is done by simple call from the server when the card is inserted in a terminal. This top-up is limited to the amount you have set. Your employees benefit from discounts at Blue Elephant centers. The availability of statistical reports via a dedicated extranet allows you to monitor the washing consumption of your fleet in real time. Simplified management You no longer need to process expense reports or advance cash: Billing at the end of the month is done on the basis of the washing services consumed during the past month. A single summary invoice for the your entire fleet is provided to you in order to simplify the administrative management of your washing station. Customizable with a tailor-made service Refillable amounts are free and can be different depending on the function of your employees You can also personalize your Pro Elephant Blue Cards with your logo. You can see directly on our map, geolocating yourself, the washing centers accepting the Blue Elephant Pro Card. I would like to have more information on the pro washing card by completing the form.
