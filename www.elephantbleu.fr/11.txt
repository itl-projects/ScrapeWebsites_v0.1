Rim Washer Rim Washer The rims are the part of the vehicle that concentrates the most dirt and the most stubborn. It is the catalyst for the most polluting dust: in addition to the burned hydrocarbons and various dust scattered all over the body, the rim receives heavy metals from the wear of the brake pads. Blue Elephant has developed a washing option specific capable of giving your rims their shine. Easy to access and use, ergonomic, the low-pressure jet rim washer is extremely effective in washing your rims. Ask those who have tried it and test it! By launching the wheel rim washer option, Blue Elephant has taken a new step and confirms its advances in environmental protection. By using this service, you make your contribution by avoiding spreading polluting substances fixed on vehicles in nature. At the slightest downpour, they run off on the side of the road and into the water table. On the Blue Elephant washing tracks, hydrocarbons, metallic particles, heavy metals and other harmful residues are entrained in the sludge and hydrocarbon separators of the washing center and then collected by an approved body. the rims deep and leaves no chance for the most stubborn dirt. Small tip and big Plus, the rim wash product is sprayed with a low pressure lance. You don't have to bend down and don't get splashed! For added safety, the product is sprayed at low pressure, thus preventing splashes on the skin and eyes. 4 tips for a perfect result: Use the rim washer before washing, on dry rims for maximum efficiency! With the dedicated lance, apply a single spray on dry rims. Let the product act during the pre -washing and washing the car you perform with the main lance. At the end of the wash, rinse the entire vehicle, including the rims. The rim wash is the first step in a successful outdoor motorcycle or car wash.
