Sales area Sales areaFill lots of friendly advice in the shop of your car wash, to maintain and wash your car at the best value for money! In addition to offering a full range of products that are among the most efficient on the market, the "Sales" area is a place of conviviality and sharing, a place of exchange between motorists and Blue Elephant, but also between scrubbers. It is in this space that technical advice on washing and practical information are transmitted to achieve daily water savings. Close to the "Sale" space, you will find an automatic distributor to buy tokens or recharge your washing key by credit card. In some Blue Elephant stations, a shop offers additional washing products and the sale of washing keys. Discover our range of washing products available in stores (depending on the equipment of your Blue Elephant washing station) Windshield glass cleaner (sprayer). Insect and droppings cleaner (sprayer). Engine cleaner (sprayer). plastic (strawberry, vanilla scent, fruity scent). Odor destroyer. Carpet stain remover. Multi-purpose cleaner. Glass microfiber / mosquito repellent. Indoor and outdoor microfiber. The Blue Elephant ancillary washing products allow you to maintain the cleanliness of your vehicle all year long between your passages on the High Pressure washing tracks or on the automatic washing gantry. Some products will keep the interior of your car always clean. Others will protect your bodywork and prolong the effects of exterior washing, always respecting the vehicle bodywork. Tip: Do not hesitate to ask your Blue Elephant car wash manager for advice.If your rims are dirty, consider using the rim washer and you will get a nickel result. And with our 470 stations in France, there is bound to be one near you. Consult the geolocation map to go to the car wash nearest to you. Did you know that the car wash stations can accommodate two-wheelers on the Blue Elephant washing tracks?
