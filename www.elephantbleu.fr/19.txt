Since 2001, the Group's headquarters have been located in Hoerdt, in Alsace, 15 minutes from Strasbourg. We are No. 1 and co-leader in France and Switzerland in vehicle washing with more than 520 washing centers totaling more than 2,500 high-pressure tracks and 300 automatic gantries. The group is positioning its services and its Blue Elephant brand on the Premium market. Its commitment to sustainable development and the preservation of the environment has been constant since its creation. The Blue Elephant brand has a very strong reputation of 95% (CSA 2017 study). Owned by the Group, it is registered worldwide and also operated by licensed partners. Our job and our vocation Car wash, in specialized stations under the Blue Elephant brand: By providing the client with a complete wash service in the best conditions of practicality, comfort and pleasure. By mastering the selection of sites and their evolution, as well as the best commercial and technical solutions for all networks. By providing investor clients with the materials, methods and reputation that allow them to recoup their investment and run the Franchisee Network. Our ambition To be the benchmark in car wash in France and Switzerland.
