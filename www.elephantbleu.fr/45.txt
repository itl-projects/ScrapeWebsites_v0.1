Respect for the bodywork The Blue Elephant difference is measured by all its exclusive technical features developed to ensure professional washing and preserve the bodywork, whatever the system used. Contactless washing and automatic washing with the exclusive brush prewash patent are guaranteed "zero micro-scratches" on the bodywork. The soap used, without any aggressive agent for the bodywork or the environment, is an exclusive Blue Elephant. use of demineralized rinsing water to avoid all traces of lime, is an exclusive Blue Elephant. The high pressure system allows precise and quality washing without contact with the body. The water pressure is perfectly calibrated to be effective without attacking the paint. The patented mixed automatic washing portal provides for pre-washing the brushes at high pressure between each washing. In emulsified foam, the brushes are soft and adherent. This exclusive process makes it possible to preserve the bodywork from any scratch caused by gravel or dirt trapped in the lamellas of the roller. pressure to rid the body of residues which could scratch it during the passage of the roller. Gentle washing guaranteed! The high pressure wash Blue Elephant is the fruit of several years of development and tests. They have helped develop the perfect combination of nozzle adjustment, jet pressure, water temperature and soap dosage to ensure safe and efficient washing. The high pressure lends itself perfectly to washing cars, motorcycles, caravans and utility vehicles. Consult the geolocation map to go to the station closest to you.
