The first branch in Berlin opened a year later. The company has been offering automotive services and accessories for over 40 years. High quality and customer satisfaction are top priorities for pit stop. The long-term loyalty of the more than one million customers proves their satisfaction with the company's services. The company now has over 320 branches across Germany and employs around 1,400 people as mechatronics, mechanics and master car mechanics. Our employees are characterized by customer friendliness and competence. Of course, advice from one of our car mechanics is always possible.
