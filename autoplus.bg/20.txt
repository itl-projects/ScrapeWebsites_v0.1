Everyone drives with Osram Choose your butter Previous Next You know us. Auto Plus Bulgaria AD is one of the leading Bulgarian companies for import and trade in auto parts. The main activity of the joint stock company is the sale of spare parts, original spare parts and oils for all car models, and the goal is to offer customers the maximum choice for each brand and model presented on the Bulgarian market. The company has a central warehouse in Sofia, as well as several regional warehouses in Plovdiv, Stara Zagora, Varna and Ruse. The successfully established distribution network, which unites 37 stores, of which 22 under its own brand "Auto Plus" and 15 franchises, covering the entire country, allows the company to fulfill the orders of its customers in a short time, thus fulfilling one of The principles that determine the company's attitude to its partners, namely - fast delivery. Professional customer service and meeting their needs are the guiding principles for Auto Plus, but no less important for the company is human. th factor. The company employs more than 200 employees who periodically go through seminars and trainings conducted jointly with the company's partners in order to increase their professional competence, develop their knowledge and skills and awareness of innovative products and technologies. The wide range of products, the guaranteed quality, the favorable price-quality ratio, the fast delivery, the professional service and the satisfaction of the needs of the client are the principles from which Auto Plus is guided in its relations with its partners!
