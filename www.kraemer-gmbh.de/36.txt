But it also ensures low noise pollution and, last but not least, guarantees that your engine is used to the full. It usually doesn't take long from the first rust stain to rusting through. The consequences: unpleasant noise, low engine power and higher fuel consumption, which is why you should have the exhaust system checked at least once a year. What does a good exhaust service include? Inspection for main inspection in accordance with § 29 StVZO for suitability Visual inspection of the entire exhaust system Repair of damaged areas If necessary Replacement of defective components Exhaust gas analysis (optional) Particulate filter installation (optional) All advantages at a glance Lower costs through: Lower fuel consumption Particulate filter retrofit More driving comfort through: Reliable exhaust and catalytic converter performance Full engine power If necessary Retrofitting a soot particle filter and thus free travel in many large cities (environmental zones) More safety through: Lowest possible pollutant emissions Lowest possible noise pollution Make an appointment with us today!
