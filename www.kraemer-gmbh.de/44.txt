By providing and networking innovative products and services, customers have easy, anywhere, anytime access to liquidity. FAQ - hire purchase by easyCredit What is hire purchase by easyCredit? With installment purchase by easyCredit, you have the option of paying conveniently and easily in installments. You decide how much you want to pay and enjoy the greatest flexibility in realizing your wishes. Who can complete the hire purchase by easyCredit? If you can have a regular monthly income, TeamBank will be happy to check your individual wish. Which documents are necessary for the order? You will receive a decision as to whether it is possible to buy in installments by easyCredit as part of the ordering process. A subsequent test is no longer necessary, so you do not have to submit any documents. What financing amounts are possible when buying in installments by easyCredit? You can make purchases between 200, - EUR to 10,000, - easily and conveniently by buying in installments by easyCredit. What terms are offered? Maturities are between 6 and 60 months. What security is required? Collateral does not have to be provided. When are the monthly installments debited? The first installment will be collected no earlier than 30 days after completion. The move-in date is automatically set to the 1st of a month. Changes to the 15th of a month are possible at any time during the term in your easyCredit customer portal. Are special repayments possible during the term? Special repayments are possible at any time free of charge. Special payments automatically reduce the monthly rate. Please note that the minimum rate of EUR 20.00 cannot be undercut. Does the refusal of my financing request affect further orders? No, your product request is always checked individually, so that a later decision can be positive. Does the PostIdent procedure have to be carried out? No, the PostIdent procedure is not necessary. Can I adjust my installment plan during the term? Should there be financial bottlenecks during the term, there is the possibility of a rate suspension or a permanent reduction in the rate. Simply visit your easyCredit customer portal and make the desired adjustment yourself. What is the easyCredit customer portal? When paying with installment purchase by easyCredit, you will automatically receive the access data to your personal easyCredit customer portal. Here you can manage your installment purchases, make changes to your personal data, place orders or even execute them yourself.
