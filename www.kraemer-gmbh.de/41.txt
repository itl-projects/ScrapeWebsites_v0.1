You have the choice whether you choose extra-light heating oil standard DIN51603-1, extra-light low-sulfur heating oil or the low-sulfur premium heating oil. We would be happy to advise you on the advantages of different heating oil qualities. Heating cleverly with premium quality ... Compared to standard heating oil, the "low sulfur EL heating oil" only has a sulfur content of at most 50 mg / kg. Among other things, this ensures clean combustion and minimization of pollutant emissions. Our premium heating oil is even more powerful and economically efficient. It also reduces deposits on the boiler walls, protects against the formation of sediments in the oil tank, counteracts clogging of filters and nozzles and thus reduces maintenance costs and trouble-free long-term operation of your system.
