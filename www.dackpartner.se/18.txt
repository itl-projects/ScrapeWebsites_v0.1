How do I know that my winter tires are approved as winter tires? Read answer Answer: Definition of winter tires is: "Tires specially designed for winter driving and marked M + S, M.S., M&S, M-S, MS or 'Mud and Snow'. The tire industry has compiled lists of tires that meet these requirements. STRO's list of winter tires that meet the Swedish Road Administration's requirements can be found on our website under the heading Winter tires Read more Hide answer? EU tire labeling what is it? How to avoid water planning? Read answer Answer: The most common reason for water leveling is too low pattern depth, if you want to avoid water leveling then your best insurance is a proper pattern depth, it is the pattern that penetrates and drains away the water so you get a secure grip on the ground.
