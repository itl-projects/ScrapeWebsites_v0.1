Overhaul Home - Overhaul Entrust your car overhaul to Point S for a complete and 100% reliable check A regular overhaul is necessary to maintain your car in accordance with the manufacturer's recommendations. This essential step ensures your safety and guarantees the proper functioning of the vehicle. The overhaul can identify possible malfunctions or wear points, and reduce the risks associated with an accumulation of problems and all the costs involved. Depending on the vehicle, the overhaul must be carried out every 15,000 to 30,000 km or every year. By respecting the maintenance schedule fixed by the manufacturer, you extend the service life of the car and you increase your chances of passing without technical inspection!
