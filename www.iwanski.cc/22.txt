Nevertheless, there may be risks that cannot be completely avoided when using these tools. In order to minimize these risks, the operator ID for working platforms (also referred to in the industry as “lift platform driving license”, “lifting platform license”) was introduced. The SYSTEM-LARD from SYSTEM-LIFT includes theoretical and practical training as well as an individual course for specific types of lifting platforms.
