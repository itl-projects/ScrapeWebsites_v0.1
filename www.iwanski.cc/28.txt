Quality-tested partner companies In order to keep the quality standard of our services high for you, our partner rental companies are checked unannounced every two years according to high criteria. The best companies are awarded the SYSTEM LIFT quality trophy. Labeling All companies that meet the quality standard may use our quality label on and within the company to signal that they meet the requirements. Inspection and exclusion Companies that do not meet the required standard will be inspected for a fee within one year. If the company does not achieve the required quality score during the inspection, this company must leave the SYSTEM LIFT network. Results of the tests The results of the quality checks and the quality ranking will be announced within our partner companies at the conference following our Annual General Meeting, where our best companies are also honored and honored. Inspection criteria Quality is the sum of the optimal interaction of all customer-relevant components. Assessment criteria of the Q-Check 2018: • Representation as a system lift partner • Organization • Customer instruction • Condition of the rental equipment • Equipment and service • Occupational safety and environment • Customer loyalty / customer satisfaction • Sales / customer advice • Minimum requirements SYSTEM-CARD training center re-audit & termination Companies that do this Failing the audit (passing threshold = at least 75% of the planned number of points) will be given the chance to demonstrate efficient improvement measures in a follow-up audit. Repeated failure leads to a termination procedure, which is accompanied by the advisory board of AVS System Lift AG.
