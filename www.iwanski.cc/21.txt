Operator training for telescopic forklifts according to DGUV principle 308-009 for all-terrain telescopic forklifts (also known as "telescopic forklift", "telescopic loader" or "teleloader") according to the step-by-step model of the training principle. Training in theory and practice be carried out using our SYSTEM-CARD e-learning. You need the SYSTEM-CARD! Training is provided in theory and practice as required for: Industrial trucks All-terrain telescopic forklifts The card is in German-English version for presentation abroad. Training content Accident events and avoidance Legal requirements and areas of responsibility Structure and function of industrial trucks and telescopic handlers Handling and handling of various attachments Safe device operation Instruction on the device, including operational driving exercises with testing Handling different loads Special use (company-specific, in consultation) Holistic training concept: blended learning SYSTEM-CARD Initial training in theory and practice depending on the selected category Duration 1 day statutory annual instruction, e.g. via e-learning Repeat training after a refreshment interval Intention of our training courses: For the professional and effective handling of telescopic forklifts and other industrial trucks To avoid accidents and site closures To fulfill the entrepreneurial obligations The training is prescribed by a trade association. Telescopic forklifts in particular require special training! NEW: Training for all-terrain telehandlers according to the new DGUV principle 308-009!
