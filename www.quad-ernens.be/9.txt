favoritesPrintPaddock CleanerDescriptionThe Wessex MTX120E is a "must" for maintaining a clean and neat paddock. He will not only collect horse dung, but also dead grass, foliage, twigs and other litter. Regular maintenance of the paddock with the Wessex MTX120E will promote the growth of a fresh and healthy grass and thus provide an advantage that a dung vacuum cleaner cannot offer you. Powerful sweeping The rotors consist of four tough polypropylene brushes, encased in a rigid plastic band. The generous length of the bristles will effortlessly send all the trash into the bin. The brush seals are fixed in pairs on the metal part of the rotor and are easily replaceable in the event of wear.More details Reference: MTX120E Quantity: This product is not sold individually.
