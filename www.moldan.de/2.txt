Contact Moldan GmbH You are here: Moldan GmbH / About us Moldan GmbH has been active as a craft and master company in Erlangen since 1949 and has been convincing since then with its high quality and outstanding services. On a plot of over 9200 square meters, we are a reliable partner in the areas of accident repair, value retention, painting and SPOT repair. But we do not have success at all costs - the trust of our customers and the quality of our services are the focus of our work. More than 32 qualified employees are responsible for the "good work" at Moldan GmbH, including master craftsmen from the areas of body construction, painting and mechanics.
