EVENTS The VLN Endurance Championship at the Nürburgring has existed since the year 1977 and it takes place on the Nürburgring-Nordschleife course. The popular sports racing series was known as the Endurance Cup until 2000. Drivers with respectively different types of cars compete in various classes - from common passenger cars to fully developed race cars. Depending on the event, the times of the races vary between 4 and 6 hours. During the competitions, there are more than 170 vehicles on the course - but drivers usually take turns.
