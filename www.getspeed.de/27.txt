The GetSpeed Performance Center is looking back at 20 years of motorsports experience including countless victories on the Nürburgring-Nordschleife – and it continues to be on the passing lane. Our mission: The professional management of sports and race drivers. Our vision: Getting the maximum of performance through innovation and passion. As passionate motorsports enthusiasts, we offer our expertise in all areas relating to racing. Whether tuning, hosting or coaching – GetSpeed is a strong partner for club and motorsports customers. Our legendary Race Taxi is also very popular: Racing fans go on a lap of the Nordschleife with our pros – in under 8 minutes! This speed will fasten your pulse more than a roller coaster – guaranteed! Adam Osieka is not only the CEO of GetSpeed, but himself a passionate and successful racing athlete. As a 20-time Class Champion at the VLN and the 24-hr race, he has a long and professional racing career to show for. For the Race Taxi, Adam Osieka and further pilots will be at your side as reliable and safe race drivers. In addition, GetSpeed is developing new technologies and sets standards in motorsports: Stress level monitoring, in cooperation with a university, and LTE live onboard streaming with Vodafone are two of the projects that we have already implemented. GetSpeed offers all from one source – from the perfect racetrack performance and local accommodation to vehicles and personal driver support. GetSpeed Performance GmbH & Co.
