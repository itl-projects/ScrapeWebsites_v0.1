Vehicle technology is becoming increasingly complex due to the increasing amount of electronics, the training required, up-to-date service provider information, and modern diagnostic systems. Bosch established the AutoCrew network in Germany in 2009, but is also constantly expanding internationally. The name AutoCrew means full service. AutoCrew workshops offer a cross-brand and knowledgeable service to car owners that satisfies every aspect of vehicle maintenance and repair. Bosch provides its partners with state-of-the-art workshop equipment, technical information, access to spare parts and a unified external and marketing look. The quality assurance system provided by Bosch protects the high level of service, which is based on regular inspections of network partners. AutoCrew services combine service expertise with Bosch experience. They are characterized by the latest diagnostic equipment, quality service, customer-centric attitude and professional work.
