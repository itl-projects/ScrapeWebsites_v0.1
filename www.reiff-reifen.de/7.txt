Make your life easier and safer and let yours Fill tires with REIFF AIRSTOPP the next time you change tires. AIRSTOPP is a clean nitrogen gas that does not burn and is non-toxic. This gas escapes much more slowly than air and thus keeps the tire pressure constant over a long period of time.
