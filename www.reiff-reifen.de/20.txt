conveniently at the branch The REIFF branches offer you many payment options! You pay for your goods directly at the branch you selected as the delivery address in your order. In the REIFF Reifen und Autotechnik branches you can pay in cash, with an EC card, by credit card or by financing. Cash payment You pay for your order at the REIFF Reifen und Autotechnik branch in EURO. EC card payment At Electronic Cash, your goods are paid for by card payment and the PIN is entered by the cardholder. Credit card The order is conveniently settled using your credit card. Your credit card details will be requested after the ordering process via a secure connection. Your credit card will only be charged with the invoice amount after receipt of the goods. Financing You can also finance the tires, rims and complete wheels you have ordered in our REIFF tires and car technology branches. Financing is concluded with Targo Bank and currently runs at an effective annual interest rate of 0.0%, the term can be individually agreed. To conclude financing, please contact our local staff in the branch. REIFF locations Find REIFF locations near you!
