When you visit our website, this data is collected automatically. Why do we collect your data? Our goal is the smooth use of our website. Cookies are text files that are automatically saved on your computer (in the browser) and contain information to recognize them when you visit the website again. A distinction can be made between "session cookies" and "tracking cookies". "Session cookies" are active for the duration of a website visit and are then deleted again. "Tracking cookies" remain for a longer period and can be used There is also the option to prevent cookies from being saved. You can find the corresponding option in your browser settings. Data collection on our website log files The website operator or the provider automatically collects and stores data about access to the offer in server log files. These server log files save the IP address, accessed web resources, time stamp, amount of data, browser type with version, operating system, URL of the previously visited page and provider and host name of the accessing computer in the form of a log.
