We offer a wide range of gardening and landscaping services, from building fences or playgrounds, to maintaining sports turf and planning objects and open spaces, right through to our own tree nurseries. Our subsidiaries based in Bremen, Braunschweig, Cuxhaven, Hamburg, Hanover, Magdeburg, Osnabrück, Walsrode and Wolfsburg provide extensive green and open space management, including planning and maintenance work.
