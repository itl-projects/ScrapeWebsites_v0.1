Home / Croatia Croatia With your own motorcycle in Croatia with Limbächer and Speer motorcycle tours. The winding coastal road offers fantastic views of mountains, islands and the sea! A side trip to Albania becomes a journey back in time. Date: September 13th to 20th, 2020 Motorbike tours with training character, driving analyzes by experienced tour guides. Together with Speer-Motorradreisen we have several motorcycle adventure tours in our program for 2019. Become part of an unforgettable motorcycle trip: get off for a week. With a lot of sun, great asphalt, low-traffic roads that wind almost endlessly through a wonderful landscape and also improve your driving style. Ride at your favorite pace on your own motorcycle In a small group of friends All groups are accompanied by experienced tour guides. During our “cappuccino stops”, the guides give helpful tips that can be put into practice right away - as you know, you never learn from fun and safety technology ... We drive in small groups of 6 people, from relaxed to sporty. A change of group is possible at any time, driving is in your personal comfort zone. The daily tours are between 250 and 350 km, we rest at a particularly beautiful location every 80 to 100 kilometers. This experience is rounded off in the evening in a cozy atmosphere in a typical atmosphere - a paradise for enthusiastic motorcyclists. All this just an hour's flight from Stuttgart. The bikes travel comfortably ahead by truck • Overnight in Rijeka, Split (2), Dubrovnik and Montenegro (3) • Luggage is transported when changing hotels! What to expect: After Rijeka, the well-developed coastal road begins with great combinations of curves in a beautiful landscape. With each curve, the perspective of the sea and islands changes. There are worthwhile views of the wild karst landscape. Behind the sea with the white rocky islands and the blue bays. In the evening we stroll through historic cities and let ourselves be pampered in small restaurants. A short journey back in time begins in Montenegro and increases in Albania. We are accompanied by a Mediterranean climate and fantastic scenery.
