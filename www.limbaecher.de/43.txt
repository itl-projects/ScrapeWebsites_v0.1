Ducati Testride is the opportunity for everyone who has always wanted to drive a Ducati Supersport, Streetfighter, XDiavel, Scrambler, Hypermotard, Monster 1200, Multistrada 1260 or Panigale ... During the course of the year, we will offer several dates under the motto of experiencing Ducati, with 8 participants each enjoying the feeling of Ducati every Saturday from 2 p.m. to 6 p.m. Please note that you should be present about 45 minutes before departure for data collection and instruction. Note: Vehicles in the upper performance classes should only be moved by experienced drivers who have an open driving license and sufficient experience. Guided tours with… 8 participants Test up to four different models A final drink in our bistro Costs Saturdays, EUR 99.00 All day on Sundays, EUR 249.00 Including fuel. On request, fully comprehensive insurance with 1000.– SB for a surcharge of EUR 20.
