This polishing agent can be processed both manually and mechanically. Effortlessly and safely removes oxidation and minor paint defects in the paint. The product has so-called 'diminishing abressives', which is a polishing grain that breaks down completely. This product is safe to use and leaves a nice finish. Comes in a 473ml bottle. Application: - Polishing and freshening up the paintwork. The advantages and properties: - A soft polishing agent to remove contamination from paint, to restore color (pigmentation) and revive the shine - Removes imperfections, polishing circulation, water stains and light oxidation - Acrylic base, so no wax, silicone and fillers in the product - Efficient and easy to use - Processing instructions are clearly stated on the packaging - Complies with VOC standard (environmentally friendly) Operating instructions manually: * For best results, work in the shade and on a cool surface. * Shake the product well before use. * Place a dot product for 10 euro cents on a foam applicator pad. * Rub the product with firm pressure in circulating motion on the paint layer. * The product is finished when the white look has disappeared and a petrolatum-like substance remains. * Wipe the residue with a clean microfiber cloth. Machine instructions: * For best results, work in the shade and on a cool surface. * Shake the product well before use. * Place 3 dots of product for 10 euro cents on a polishing pad. * Spread the product at the lowest speed over an area of ​​40x40 cm. * Then process the product between 1000 - 1500 RPM, depending on the paint condition. * The product is finished when the white look has disappeared and a petrolatum-like substance remains. * Wipe the residue with a clean microfiber cloth.
