It is designed to meet the highest protection requirements. Thanks to its unique composition, the varnish and paint of your vehicle will be more resistant to micro-scratches, to road film and the maintenance of your car will be easier. . Ceramics are extremely resistant to weather conditions, acid rain, bird droppings, washing, even automatic rollers and many chemicals. It also provides an extremely hydrophobic effect and a more intense color. With proper care, this coating can easily protect your vehicle for a minimum of 3 years. On a new vehicle the ceramic can be applied directly after steam cleaning of the bodywork and the rims. In the case of a second-hand vehicle, it is imperative to practice beforehand, a pre-wash with water of the bodywork + a decontamination with a clay bar + an integral polishing of the bodywork and a steam cleaning. The service requires up to 4 days of immobilization of the vehicle.
