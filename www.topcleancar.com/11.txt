Take advantage of the Donation collection offer for the association Soleil d'Enfance since early September 2014, we have been in partnership with the association Soleil d'Enfance. Your Top Clean Car cleaning company in turn contributes by committing € 1 to the association for each service provided. * Founded in 1972 by Felix Marouani, the Sun Childhood Association aims to improve the daily lives of children with disabilities. Your Top Clean Car cleaning company in turn contributes by committing € 1 to the association for each service provided. Mission Fundraising is the main goal of the Association Soleil d'Enfance. Which funds are then redistributed to various centers, among others through the purchase of vehicles for the transportation of children. For the past 40 years, nearly 233 minibuses have been offered, or around 3,500,000 euros distributed. Description Recognized association of public interest, Soleil d´Enfance brings together the worlds of entertainment, communication, circus, sport and, its main purpose is the permanent search for funds intended to help children in their daily lives. Soleil d´Enfance also organizes various events throughout the year such as concerts, theater or circus evenings, humor festivals, etc. Soleil d'Enfance strives to continue the work of its founder Félix Marouani. Artists, authors, directors, producers, technicians form the big family of the Show and by their achievements are "sowers of happiness" for children.
