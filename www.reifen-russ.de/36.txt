SUVs combine top-class driving comfort with powerful drive power and great ground clearance. The all-round talents can be driven in city traffic as well as on long journeys and off the beaten track. For frequent drivers who enjoy occasional trips into the terrain, the SUV is a sign of freedom and driving pleasure. Sport utility vehicles and other off-road vehicles have to meet higher tire requirements than conventional road vehicles. SUVs are usually designed as all-wheel drive vehicles for optimal power transmission in adverse road conditions. As a result, the front and rear tires are equally loaded and should each have a suitable profile for difficult surfaces. Large tread blocks with strong ties to the ground are required to ensure maximum driving pleasure when driving through deep mud, fine sand or over pointed stones. In our specialist workshop we regularly equip SUVs and other off-road vehicles with high quality tires for driving off the paved roads. Our specialists have extensive expertise in handling tires for off-road vehicles and are happy to explain the advantages and disadvantages of various off-road tires. If you are looking for a competent partner for off-road tires, we will be happy to help you. How to find the perfect tires for your SUV Depending on the intended use, different types of tires can be used for your SUV. For normal road cars that are only used on well-paved roads, so-called all-weather tires for cross-season use are offered in addition to summer and winter tires. Various off-road tires are also available for off-road vehicles. For occasional off-road drivers, all-terrain tires are particularly suitable, which are equipped with a solid AT profile and can be used both on the road and in light terrain. The AT profile represents a good compromise between off-road and on-road tires and convinces with low fuel consumption on the road and solid grip off-road. If you are mainly on demanding terrain off-road, you should use mud-terrain tires with a special MT tread. The MT profile was specially designed for muddy, wet and slippery surfaces. MT tires are more rough compared to AT tires and can therefore also be used in very demanding areas. In addition to the AT and MT tires for SUVs, off-road vehicles and commercial vehicles, we also offer our customers other special tires. These are suitable, for example, for particularly demanding trips through the desert or can even be used in competitions. We will advise you personally and competently in our specialist workshop when looking for the right tire for your SUV.
