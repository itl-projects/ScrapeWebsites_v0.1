Tailor-made tires for our conditions When buying tires, you should choose tires that are developed for the driving conditions you most often encounter. This is especially important when buying winter tires. If you drive on changing rides, ice or hard packed snow in winter, studded tires or Nordic studded winter tires are a safer choice. Central European tire-free winter tires do not meet the requirements of winter in the Nordic countries for tires. When it comes to winter tires, the tire marking does not help Nordic consumers. It can even be misleading and jeopardize traffic safety. The tire marking applies to stud-free winter tires but not stud tires.Test resultThe best Nordic stud-free winter tires had more than 18 meters shorter braking distance (braking from 80 km / h) than the best Central European stud-free winter tires (Magazine Tekniikan Maailma 19/2011). between tire-free tires When buying tires, you should choose tires that are designed for the most common driving conditions.
