GO! Our partners: trailers, semitrailers and construction machinery Company Nufa, a company of the Gassmann Group, was founded in 1974 by Mr. Alfred Gassmann. The management has been in the hands of Mr. Jens-Wolfgang Engelage since 2006. Around 300 commercial vehicles as well as trailers, semitrailers and construction machines of all kinds, new and used, are always for sale on more than 34,000 m², approx. 1/3 of the space being provided with halls. Due to the own workshop operation and as a service base of DEKRA a short-term delivery of the vehicles is possible, on request also with a new AU-HU acceptance. Also in the area of ​​possible repairs or repair or preparation work, there are no unnecessary delays for the customer due to the long-standing employees since everything is done on site without detours. Most of the vehicles are always prepared and checked and can be delivered on the same day if desired. Global customers, in particular those from other European countries, appreciate this because they do not have any unnecessary waiting times. As specialists in the field of export / import of vehicles of all kinds, our employees are able to prepare all possible formalities for the customer in order to enable them to be dispatched smoothly and imported quickly. Our sales team will help you with advice and action for all your questions in the area of ​​your transport tasks. If the corresponding vehicle with a total stock of more than 2,000 units is really not to be found in our group of companies, there is guaranteed to be a solution like adapting another vehicle to your needs. Modifications or changes to the structure are possible without major problems.
