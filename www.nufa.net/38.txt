Since 2005 he has been in charge of the management of the same Jens-Wolfgang Engelage. Around 300 vehicles with trailers, gondolas and construction machines of all kinds, both new and second-hand, are always exposed for sale for more than 34,000 m², despite the fact that a a third of it is occupied by ships. An own workshop and the DEKRA service point enable short-term vehicle deliveries, carrying out new tests for contamination and gas emission whenever desired. There are also no delays due to possible repairs, maintenance or set-up services thanks to our qualified staff who have been providing these services on site for our customers for years, thus avoiding unnecessary waste of time. Most vehicles are always ready and serviced, and on request, same day delivery can be made. Customers from all over the world, especially those from the rest of Europe, know how to appreciate this fact, so they don't have to wait unnecessarily. As specialists in the export-import sector of vehicles of all kinds, our employees can prepare all the formalities for customers in order to enable smooth shipping and quick importation. In the event that the corresponding vehicle could not really be found in the total stock of more than 2,000 units available to our group of companies, there will surely be a solution, such as the possibility of transforming another vehicle to adapt it to your needs.
