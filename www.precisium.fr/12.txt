Appearance of a new type of failure: the so-called "critical" failure on the vital functions of the vehicle. In the event of a "critical" failure, driving with your vehicle will only be authorized until midnight on the same day. Beyond this period, the vehicle will no longer be authorized to be moved. Repairs must be made within two months. Find all the information concerning the new technical control on the government site: https://www.service-public.fr/particuliers/vosdroits/F2878 Drinking and driving: Small Reminder: "In France, it is forbidden to drive with a blood alcohol level greater than 0.5 g of alcohol per liter of blood (0.2 g / l for probationary permits). »« The maximum alcohol level is reached: ½ hour after absorption on an empty stomach 1 hour after absorption during a meal. The blood alcohol level drops on average from 0.10 g to 0.15 g of alcohol per liter of blood in 1 hour. Salt coffee, spoonful of oil…: there is no “trick” to eliminate alcohol more quickly. Please note: some medications may be incompatible with alcohol consumption. Read the instructions carefully or ask your doctor for advice in case of doubt. »Find all the information on alcohol and driving on the road safety website: http://www.securite-routiere.gouv.fr/connaitre-les-regles/reglementation-et-sanctions/alcool Triangle et vest Mandatory yellow: Since February 13, 2008, you are required to have a safety vest and a warning triangle in your vehicle. Attention, in the event of non-compliance with this obligation, you incur a fixed fine of 135 €! The same goes for drivers of 2 and 3 wheels. Since 2016, you must also have a yellow vest in your vehicle.
