Your tires In winter temperatures drop and grip on the roads becomes difficult. It is therefore advisable to equip yourself with winter tires or all-season tires to ride more serenely. Your battery Your battery is also more stressed due to the more frequent use of your headlights, ventilation to remove fogging or even your windshield wipers. To not break down, do not hesitate to ask your repairer to check it . The latter will tell you whether to recharge or replace. Your headlights In winter you have to see and be seen! Check the condition and setting of your headlights and check that your bulbs are working properly. If you still have a question or a doubt, contact your nearest Precisium!
