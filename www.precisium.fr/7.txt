This is an obligation imposed by law, under penalty of a fine, and in certain cases a ban on driving with the automobile. All private and commercial vehicles weighing less than 3.5 tonnes are affected: in the 6 months preceding the 4th anniversary of their first entry into service, then every 2 years for a private vehicle and every year for a commercial vehicle. In the event of resale or transaction, a technical inspection of less than 6 months must be presented at the prefecture for the new owner when requesting a change of registration card.The good condition of your vehicle conditions the result of the inspection.CAUTION: On May 20, 2018, the Technical Inspection greatly evolved with the appearance of a new notion of critical failures. In this case, the vehicle is no longer authorized and is no longer insured from midnight on the day of the inspection. HOW DO I KNOW THAT THE TECHNICAL CHECK MUST BE DONE? Refer to your registration card on which the date of validity of your technical inspection is noted, or on the windshield sticker for vehicles over 4 years old. WHAT TO DO? Ask for an appointment with your Précisium repairer so that he can pre-check your vehicle!
