Vibrations of the steering wheel are a sign of malfunctions in the steering system, so be careful. Several sources can explain these vibrations : A presence of games in one of the front axles, Degradation of the chassis, Poor restoration following an accident, One of your tires is deflated, One or more of your wheels are incorrectly mounted or veiled, A problem with parallelism, Poor wheel balance adjustment. What to do: Ask the advice of your local repairer, Maintain your wheels, steering and suspension systems, and the components of your brakes frequently. How do I start my car when my battery is flat? Use starter cables: Ask for the help of another motorist to be able to connect you with the help of starter cables on its battery, in order to give a boost to your battery and to be able to restart it while you go at your local repairer. A rain treatment for my Windshield, is it really useful? Anti-rain treatments for windshields are products that do not emulsify with water. Thanks to this treatment, raindrops can neither adhere nor spread on the windshield and clean the impacts of 'insects is much simpler.Your visibility will be improved and therefore your safety too! The treatment works on average 1 month. It must therefore be repeated frequently enough to maintain effectiveness.
