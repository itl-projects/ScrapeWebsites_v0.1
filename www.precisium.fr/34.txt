In addition, in order to preserve the air conditioning system and avoid leaks, we advise you to turn on your air conditioning every month for at least 15 minutes. The characteristic signs that allow you to detect air conditioning problems? Fogging is formed on the windshield when it is switched on A lack of cold A metallic noise when the air conditioning works To avoid unpleasant surprises as summer approaches, it is advisable to go and check with a When? : Every year: circuit cleaning with antibacterial treatment and circuit control. Every 2 years: circuit cleaning, gas refill and replacement of the cabin filter.
